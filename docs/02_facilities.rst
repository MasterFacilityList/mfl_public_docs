Locating Facilities Within the Master Facility List
====================================================

Facilities in the MFL can be located via search and geolocation.

Location Through Search
++++++++++++++++++++++++
The homepage has a search field that searches facilities by their code, name e.t.c.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/02_searching.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Searching Facilities

The results of the search are shown alongside the advanced search. Using the advanced
search the query can be fine tuned e.g. listing facilities in a certain ward.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/03_search_results.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Searching Facilities

The facility advanced search can also be accessed directly by selecting the find facilities
button in the top navigation bar.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/06_list_facilities.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Listing Facilities

The advanced search form on the left has fields that can further specify your search.

There's an export facilities option that enables users to export and download the filtered
facilities in excel format.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/07_export_facilities.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Export Facilities

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/07_b_export_facilities.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Exported Facilities


Opening one facility show more details about the facility. The basic details of the facility
are displayed on top. Below the basic details, the facility's services, location on a map,
community units and (regulation) departments are displayed in tabs.

The facility services tab shows the services that the facility offers.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_detailed_page.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View

The facility's administrative unit (county, sub-county and ward) links to geolocation of facilities
within the selected facility administrative unit.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_e_detailed_page.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View

A printable version of the facility details is available by pressing the print
button.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_f_detailed.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View

The regulation tab shows the departments that are in the facility.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_b_detailed_page.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View

The community health units tab displays the community health units that are
attached to the facility. Clicking on one unit opens up more details about
the unit.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_c_detailed_page.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View

The show facility on map tab displays the position of the facility in
the ward the facility is in.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/04_d_detailed_page.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View


Rating A Facility's Services
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
Users are able to rate facility services by selecting the view service rating
of the service to be rated. Rating is restricted to one service per day.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/05_a_rating.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Detailed Facility View


Location Through the Geo-locator
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
The geolocator is the second way of locating facilities. The geolocator is accessed
using the facility geolocator button in the top navigation. The page shows the map of
Kenya with all 47 counties. The map has a heatmap showing concentration of facilities
in an area.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/08_facility_geolocator.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Facility Geo-locator

Clicking on one county brings up the selected county's sub counties.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/08_facility_geolocator_county.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: County Drill Down

Clicking on one sub-county brings up the selected sub-county's wards.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/08_facility_geolocator_subcounty.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Sub-county Drill Down

The ward level is the lowest administrative unit available.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/08_facility_geo_locator_ward.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Ward Drill Down

On the left of the map, there's a button that can be used to view facilities
in the administrative unit, in this case, the ward.

.. figure:: /_images/facilities/08_facility_geolocator_link_to_advanced_search.png
    :scale: 100%
    :alt: Link to Advanced Search
